CC@S Community Breakfast

As disciples of Jesus, we are called to help our brothers and sisters in need. No previous cooking or serving experience is required!

  • If you are scheduled to cook, please convene at the Old Union Kitchen (first floor of OU, to the right of the door to TAP) bright and early at 7 a.m. We should have everything cooked by around 8:15 a.m.
  • If you are scheduled to serve, please come to the Old Union Kitchen (first floor of OU, to the right of the door to TAP) by 8:15 a.m. We'll serve breakfast at the Opportunity Center until 9:15 a.m., and we'll return to campus by around 9:30 a.m.
  • If you are scheduled to drive, the Opportunity Center is at 33 Encina Ave, Palo Alto, CA 94301. It's a big red building that's hard to miss. Park across the street behind the shopping center.
